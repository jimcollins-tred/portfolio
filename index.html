About / Me

I am a Manchester-based developer and designer, and throughout my career I’ve been guided by these twin passions. I’ve loved coding since I was a young boy writing text adventures in BASIC, and have always enjoyed the unique mix of creativity and problem-solving it requires. I also love a challenge. In the last year I’ve taught myself HTML5 and CSS, and am a recent graduate of Northcoders coding bootcamp, where I relished the chance to build upon my background in Computer Science and learn the latest exciting technologies.

In my design work, I have over fifteen years’ experience working on a wide variety of projects. The key to great design has always been great communication. I collaborate closely with my clients to fully understand their needs, then use attractive, considered design to deliver their message to its audience with maximum clarity and impact.

Above all I love to tell a story, be that through the websites I design and build or the fiction I write. My goal is to unite these interests, creating beautiful websites and applications that excite, inspire and inform their users.
