About / Me

I am a Manchester-based graphic designer and developer, with over twenty years’ experience working with a wide variety of clients.

Great design should stimulate, inspire and, most importantly, communicate. I collaborate closely with my clients to fully understand their needs, then use attractive, considered design to deliver their message to its audience with maximum clarity and impact.

I also love a challenge. I am currently working through a full-stack software development bootcamp at Northcoders, building on my background in computer science and augmenting my skillset with the latest, most exciting technologies.

My / Work

I’ve been a graphic designer for over fifteen years now, working with a wide variety of clients across magazines, books, advertising and other printed media. I’ve rocked out with XFM, got swept up by Beethoven and Mozart for Classic FM, and I’ve rambled with the, er, Ramblers.

I've recently rediscovered my love of coding, and am about to embark on an exciting new chapter in web design and development.
