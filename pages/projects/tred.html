Tred were a pioneering fintech, empowering businesses to track their spending and carbon footprint through a debit card and mobile app. I gained over three years' experience of commercial app development, working in a small, talented Agile team.

I played a key role in shaping our digital experiences across mobile, web, and back-end systems. Project / Spotlight

Home screen redesign

This year, we commissioned two UX designers to gather feedback on the app from our users. Based on this, I then led a complete redesign of the app home screen, from concept to implementation.

The most common complaint was that the home screen was too busy and packed with information users would not need to see upon app open. Listing open banking accounts was deemed unnecessary clutter, as was the transactions panel which took up the entire bottom half of the screen.

Users also wanted quick access to important features like their PIN and account details, and quick access to customer support.
