Wildlife Britain is a well-illustrated, full-colour guide to 1,000 of the best wildlife-rich sites across the UK.

The goal here was to marry beautiful photography with an attractive, easy-to-read design in order to inspire and inform in equal measure.

Picture research in particular was a vital part of this project.
