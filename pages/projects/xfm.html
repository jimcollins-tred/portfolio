XFM (now Radio X) is a commercial radio station with an alternative, indie rock focus.

When I came to design this compendium of their top 1000 songs of all time, I went for a distressed, grungy feel to reflect this, as well as extensive use of their excellent library of album and band photography.

This is very much the kind of music I love to listen to, making this a hugely enjoyable project to work on.

Did you know?

Franz Ferdinand’s seminal noughties classic ‘Take Me Out’ only ever appears on their setlists as ‘The Scottish Song’, in reference to Shakespearean actors superstitiously referring to Macbeth as ‘The Scottish Play’.
